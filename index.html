About Me

Hello! Welcome to my website. You probably already know, but I’m Jojo Moyes and I write novels and screenplays. Here you will be able to find out a bit more about me and my books, and any up to date news, such as tour dates. I’m very happy to say that the film adaptation of Me Before You , starring Sam Claflin and Emilia Clarke, was released on the 3rd of June 2016.
